How do odds work?
Odds represent the probability of an event happening and determine how much you can win on a bet. Decimal (Standard): Example: Odds of 2.00 indicate that you will win 2x the amount you bet (including the original amount). Fractional: Example: Odds of 3/1 indicate that you will win 3x the amount you bet, in addition to the initial amount. American: Odds +200 indicate that a profit of $200 will be made with a bet of $100; odds -150 indicate that you need to bet $150 to win $100.
